## 1. Concentration Terms
Understanding how to express concentration and interconvert between terms is foundational for physical chemistry calculations.

*   **Temperature Dependent:** 
    *   **Molarity (M):** Moles of solute divided by the volume of solution in litres.
    *   **Normality (N):** Calculated as $N = n \times M$, where $n$ is the n-factor.
*   **Temperature Independent:** 
    *   **Molality (m):** Moles of solute divided by the mass of solvent in kg.
    *   **Mole Fraction ($x_A$):** $x_A = n_A / (n_A + n_B)$.
    *   **Mass Percentage:** $(Mass\ of\ solute\ /\\ Mass\ of\ solution) \times 100$.
    *   **Parts Per Million (PPM):** $(Mass\ of\ solute\ /\\ Mass\ of\ solution) \times 10^6$.
*   **Crucial Interconversion Formula:** 
    *   Convert Molarity to Molality: $M = (m \times d \times 1000) /\\ (1000 + m \times M_2)$, where $d$ is density and $M_2$ is solute molar mass.

## 2. Henry's Law (Gas in Liquid Solutions)
*   **Formula:** $p = K_H \times x$
*   **Key Dependencies:** Higher $K_H$ = Lower Solubility. $Temp \uparrow \Rightarrow K_H \uparrow \Rightarrow Solubility \downarrow$.
*   **$K_H$ Order:** Helium > Hydrogen > Nitrogen > Oxygen.

## 3. Raoult's Law & Liquid-Liquid Solutions
*   **Ideal:** $P_A = x_A \times P^\circ_A$; $\Delta H_{mix} = 0, \Delta V_{mix} = 0$.
*   **Positive Deviation:** $P_A > x_A P^\circ_A$; $\Delta H_{mix} > 0$ (e.g., Ethanol + Water).
*   **Negative Deviation:** $P_A < x_A P^\circ_A$; $\Delta H_{mix} < 0$ (e.g., Chloroform + Acetone).

## 4. Azeotropes
*   **Min Boiling:** Large positive deviation.
*   **Max Boiling:** Large negative deviation.

## 5. Colligative Properties
*   **RLVP:** $(P^\circ - P) /\\ P^\circ = x_B$.
*   **Boiling Point Elevation:** $\Delta T_b = i \times K_b \times m$.
*   **Freezing Point Depression:** $\Delta T_f = i \times K_f \times m$.
*   **Osmotic Pressure:** $\pi = i \times C \times R \times T$.

## 6. Van't Hoff Factor ($i$)
*   $i = Observed /\\ Calculated = M_{calc} /\\ M_{obs}$.
*   **Dissociation:** $i = 1 + (n-1)\alpha$ ($i > 1$).
*   **Association:** $i = 1 - (1 - 1/n)\alpha$ ($i < 1$).
